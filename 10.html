DumbphoneBridge

Connecting your dumbphone to the world of smartphone apps.

  • Seamlessly send text messages to interact with your favorite smartphone apps.
  • Experience the convenience of app functionalities from your dumbphone.
  • Stay connected with friends and family on messaging apps like WhatsApp and Facebook Messenger.
  • Receive real-time updates and notifications from your desired apps via SMS.
  • Compatible with a wide range of dumbphones, no smartphone required.
